Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 15, 2025

Introduction

TickTime is a Chrome Extension designed to help users convert .NET ticks to DateTime, convert DateTime to ticks, and perform timezone conversions. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it.

Data Collection

TickTime does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data or user information. All operations are performed locally within your browser, and no data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

TickTime only requests the minimum permissions required to function and does not access, collect, or share any sensitive information.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or TickTime, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store listing.
