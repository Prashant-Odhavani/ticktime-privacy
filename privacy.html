Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 15, 2025

Introduction

DevTicks is a Chrome Extension built for developers to convert .NET ticks, DateTime formats, Unix timestamps, and compare multiple timezones. Your privacy is very important to us, and we are committed to keeping your data safe.

Data Collection

DevTicks does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal or usage data. All operations are performed locally within your browser, and no data is sent to external servers or third parties.

Permissions

DevTicks uses no special permissions and only accesses files and functions necessary to operate within the browser extension environment. It does not access: Browsing history, Personal files, Cookies or website data, or Network or external services.

Third-Party Services

DevTicks does not use any third-party APIs or external dependencies that transmit data. It operates in a fully secure, offline mode.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or DevTicks, please contact us via the support section of the Chrome Web Store listing.
