Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 14, 2026

Introduction

AzenTicks is a Chrome Extension built for developers to convert .NET ticks, DateTime formats, Unix timestamps, and compare multiple timezones. Your privacy is important to us, and the extension is designed to work locally in your browser.

Data Collection

AzenTicks does not collect, share, or transmit personal or usage data to us or to third parties. All conversions are performed locally within your browser.

The extension does store a small amount of data locally on your device to support the user experience, such as your selected tab, timezone preferences, saved timezone sets, and recent conversion history. This local data never leaves your browser unless you explicitly copy or export it yourself.

Permissions

AzenTicks requests no special Chrome permissions. It does not access browsing history, personal files, cookies, site data, or external network services.

Third-Party Services

AzenTicks does not use third-party APIs or external services to process your data. It can be used fully offline after installation.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or AzenTicks, please contact us via the support section of the Chrome Web Store listing.
